    Basic Notation

    In the image shown below, the white is facing up while red is facing front

    R

    Turn the right layer up

    R'

    Turn the right layer down

    R2

    Turn the right layer up 2 times

    L

    Turn the left layer down

    L'

    Turn the left layer up

    L2

    Turn the left layer up 2 times

    F

    Turn the front layer clock wise 1 time

    F'

    Turn the front layer counter clock wise 1 time

    F2

    Turn the front layer clockwise 2 times

    B

    Turn the top layer left

    B'

    Turn the top layer right

    B2

    Turn the top layer 2 times

    U

    Turn the top layer left

    U'

    Turn the top layer right

    U2

    Turn the top layer 2 times

    D

    Turn the bottom layer right

    D'

    Turn the bottom layer left

    D2

    Turn the bottom layer 2 times
